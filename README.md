# 📊 Predictive Analytics Dashboard

## 🌟 Overview
The Predictive Analytics Dashboard is an interactive Power BI project developed to transform raw sales data into meaningful business insights. It provides a comprehensive analysis of sales performance through dynamic visualizations, enabling users to identify trends, compare regional performance, monitor KPIs, and evaluate product and category-wise sales. The dashboard is designed to support data-driven decision-making with an intuitive and user-friendly interface.

## 🎯 Objectives
- Analyze overall sales performance.
- Monitor key business metrics.
- Identify yearly and monthly sales trends.
- Compare sales across different regions.
- Discover top-performing products and categories.
- Enable interactive business analysis using filters.

## 📌 Key Features
- 📈 KPI Cards (Total Sales, Average Sales, Total Orders & Total Products)
- 📅 Year-wise Sales Trend Analysis
- 📆 Month-wise Sales Performance
- 🌍 Regional Sales Comparison
- 🛍️ Category-wise Sales Distribution
- 🏆 Top 10 Best-Selling Products
- 🎛️ Interactive Slicers for Region, Category & Ship Mode
- 📊 Clean and Professional Dashboard Design

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies
- Power BI Desktop
- Power Query
- DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)
- Microsoft Excel

## 📊 Business Insights
- Total sales exceeded **2.26M**, reflecting strong business performance.
- The West region generated the highest sales compared to other regions.
- Technology emerged as the top-performing product category.
- Sales showed an increasing trend over the years.
- Interactive filters allow users to explore insights from multiple business perspectives.
